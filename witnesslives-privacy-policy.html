WitnessLives

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

WitnessLives is built on a simple principle: your family history belongs to you. All data you enter or import into the app stays on your device. We do not collect it, store it on our servers, or share it with anyone.

1. Who We Are

WitnessLives is an iOS application developed and operated by Rufus Howe ("we," "us," or "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at privacy@witnesslives.com.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials (no account is required)
  • Your family tree data, GEDCOM files, or any genealogical records you import
  • Photos or documents you add to the app
  • Search queries, temporal queries, or any other activity within the app
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
All of the above stays on your iPhone. It never leaves your device.

3. Location Data

WitnessLives uses your device's location services for specific in-app features, such as identifying ancestors buried near your current location and mapping geographic connections within your family tree.

Location data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers, associated with your identity, or shared with third parties. You can revoke location access at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → WitnessLives. Revoking location access disables proximity-based features but does not affect any other part of the app.

4. Analytics and Crash Reporting

WitnessLives does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or crash reporting SDKs. We do not use services such as Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar tools.

If you opt into Apple's standard crash reporting via your iOS system settings, Apple may share anonymised diagnostic information with us through App Store Connect. This information is provided by Apple, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, and does not include any of your personal data or family tree content.

5. Third-Party Services

WitnessLives does not integrate with, or transmit data to, any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers. The app does not contain any advertising.

6. Children's Privacy

WitnessLives is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Because no data is collected by the app at all, no special handling for children is required beyond this statement.

7. For Users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA or UK, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or UK GDPR may apply to the processing of your personal data.

Because WitnessLives processes all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to us, we do not act as a data controller or data processor with respect to your genealogical data or location data. You are the sole controller of your own data.

The only scenario in which we may receive personal data from EEA or UK users is if you contact us by email (for example, for support). In that case:

  • Data collected: your email address and any information you include in your message
  • Purpose: responding to your enquiry
  • Legal basis: legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR)
  • Retention: we retain support emails for up to 12 months, then delete them
  • Your rights: you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your data, and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@witnesslives.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. For California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information.

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal information. We do not collect personal information from users of WitnessLives beyond what you voluntarily send us by email. You may request deletion of any such email correspondence by contacting us at privacy@witnesslives.com.

9. Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the security of your family history is determined by the security of your iPhone — including your device passcode, Face ID or Touch ID, and iOS data encryption, which Apple enables by default. We encourage you to keep your device updated to the latest version of iOS.

We recommend using the app's built-in export feature to maintain a personal backup of your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. If we introduce features that change how data is handled — for example, optional cloud backup or account creation — we will update this policy and revise the effective date before those features are released. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
